FAQ

Common questions

How long does a review take? ↓
All written reviews are delivered within 3–5 business days via email. Strategy calls and mock interviews are scheduled after purchase — you'll receive a calendar link to pick your preferred time.
How do I send you my documents? ↓
After payment you'll receive a confirmation email. Reply to that email with your documents attached — CV, personal statement, application sections, or whatever is relevant to your service. You can also email directly to info@thesmatdentist.com with your order reference.
What if I need revisions after my review? ↓
Each service includes one round of feedback. If you make significant revisions and want a second pass, you're welcome to purchase the service again — or upgrade to a VIP membership which includes ongoing feedback throughout your journey.
Is payment secure? ↓
Yes. All payments are processed securely through Stripe — the same payment platform used by millions of businesses worldwide. Your card details are never stored or shared.
What's the difference between à la carte and a VIP membership? ↓
À la carte is perfect if you need help with one specific thing right now. VIP membership ($49.99 or $79.99/month) is for applicants who want ongoing support, multiple reviews per month, priority responses, and a structured mentorship relationship throughout their entire application journey.
Do you offer refunds? ↓
Due to the personalized nature of these services, all sales are final once the review or call has been delivered. If you have concerns before your review is completed, please reach out to info@thesmatdentist.com and we'll do our best to help.
